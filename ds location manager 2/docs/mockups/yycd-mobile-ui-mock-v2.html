About Our YYCD Program

La Danse Cleveland's Community Outreach — a community dance program for individuals aged 18 and older with intellectual or developmental disabilities.

Enjoy the fun of moving to music in a lively, energetic learning environment under the guidance of trained dance mentors. Dancing is the hidden language of the soul, and we're committed to enhancing the physical and emotional well-being of individuals through social dance experiences.
